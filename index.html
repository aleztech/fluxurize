Companion for n8n • iOS app

Control your n8n workflows from your iPhone

Companion for n8n is a native iOS client that connects directly to your own n8n instances, so you can monitor workflows, trigger runs and review recent executions in a few taps.

Download on the App Store  ▶ See what you can do
Works with n8n Cloud and self hosted Secure credentials stored in Keychain No external backend, no tracking

Main capabilities

  • ✓ Connect to multiple n8n instances, cloud and self hosted, with secure API credentials.
  • ✓ Browse workflows with filters and search, check status and last run at a glance.
  • ✓ Trigger manual runs from your phone when you need to kick off automation on the go.
  • ✓ Inspect recent executions to understand if a workflow is healthy or failing.
Privacy first. Connections are created directly between your device and your n8n instances. Companion for n8n does not proxy traffic, does not use a separate backend and does not collect analytics.
