What is Fluxurize Companion

Fluxurize Companion is a native iOS client for your own n8n instances. Connect your server, browse workflows and toggle them on or off in seconds.

  • Connect to your own n8n instances, no external backend.
  • See workflows grouped by status and mark favorites.
  • Trigger actions quickly from your phone when you are on the move.

Independent product: this app is an independent client, it is not affiliated with, sponsored by or endorsed by n8n GmbH.

Free vs Pro

  • Free, one instance, favorite up to 5 workflows.
  • Pro, multiple instances, unlimited favorites and more convenience features.

You can upgrade to Pro from inside the app using a secure in app purchase. Subscriptions are managed by Apple and can be cancelled at any time from your App Store account.
