FAQ

Answers to common questions

Do you proxy my traffic?

No. The app connects directly from your device to your n8n instances.

Where are my credentials stored?

In the iOS keychain. Fluxurize does not host or store them.

What kind of instances are supported?

Self hosted setups over HTTPS and n8n Cloud, as long as they are reachable from your device.

Do you track how I use the app?

No tracking SDKs. We keep analytics to a bare minimum and do not correlate your workflows.
